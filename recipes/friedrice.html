Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a quick and tasty dish made with cooked rice, vegetables, and eggs stir-fried together. It is perfect for lunch, dinner, or using up leftover rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 2 eggs
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Scramble the eggs and set aside.
  3. Add vegetables and stir fry.
  4. Add rice, soy sauce, eggs, and mix well.
