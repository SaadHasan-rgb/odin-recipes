Chicken Curry

Description

Chicken curry is a rich and aromatic dish cooked with tender chicken pieces in a blend of spices. It is a classic South Asian comfort food enjoyed with rice, roti, or paratha.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken
  • 2 onions (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Saut√© onions in oil.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook.
  3. Add tomatoes and spices.
  4. Add chicken and cook until sealed.
  5. Add water and simmer until tender.
  6. Serve warm with rice or roti.
